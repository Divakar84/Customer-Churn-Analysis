# 📊 Retail Business Performance & Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail transaction data to identify profit-draining products, optimize inventory management, and uncover key business insights. The analysis focuses on understanding how inventory behavior impacts profitability and sales performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit across categories and regions
* Identify loss-making and slow-moving products
* Study the relationship between inventory days and profitability
* Build an interactive dashboard for business insights

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data querying and aggregation
* **Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib)** – Data analysis & visualization
* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Global Superstore Dataset**
* Contains:

  * Sales, Profit, Quantity
  * Category & Sub-Category
  * Region & Customer data
  * Order Date

⚠️ *Note:* Inventory Days were not available in the dataset and were simulated using random values for analysis purposes.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes:

* 📌 KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Profit, Avg Inventory Days)
* 📊 Profit by Category
* 📈 Sales vs Profit (Scatter Plot)
* 📦 Inventory Days by Product (Slow-moving items)
* 🌍 Region-wise Sales
* 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
* 🎛 Filters (Region, Category, Year)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* High inventory days are associated with lower profitability
* Some products generate losses despite high sales
* Overstocking leads to inefficient resource utilization
* Sales performance varies across regions

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Reduce stock levels for slow-moving products
* Improve demand forecasting
* Focus on high-margin products
* Optimize pricing strategies
* Target high-performing regions
